After seeing films and photos of the poor living conditions and filthy drinking water that is a way of life for the children of Surinam, South America, Pastor W.W.Walker of Redeemed Baptist church in Landrum, SC (along with several of his congregation members) was touched and felt the need to help. They teamed up, put their heads together, and designed a method of "Washing in Wells." This shallow well digging system is easy to use and transport to the mission fields. The simple process uses only a gasoline powered pump, galvanized pipe, check valve, and an old fashioned hand pump. This method was tried and tested by a Mission Team in Surinam, South America. 

Our Mission Teams are continuously traveling throughout Africa and India to different villages digging wells, and sending shoes, clothing, and other necessities that the local children and their families need to fight disease and sicknesses. 

 

   We have no paid employees and 100% of support contributions go toward the ministry. Redeemed Baptist Church is doing all we can do with our ministry work and the work that we do with the youth in our area. Charisma and Christian Life Magazine believe strongly in what we are trying to do and wanted to help us get the word out by featuring Cup of Water Ministries in their April issue. 
  Our goal is to help children around the world and we need your prayers.

God is working with His Great and Mighty Hand!
